About H.A. IMPEX

H.A. IMPEX is a trusted Import-Export company based in Delhi, India, committed to supplying premium-quality Dry Fruits, Pulses, Rice, and Indian Spices to customers worldwide.

Trusted Global Export Partner

H.A. IMPEX is a trusted Import-Export company based in Delhi, India, dedicated to supplying premium-quality Dry Fruits, Pulses, Rice, and Indian Spices to customers across international markets. Established in 2026, the company was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between quality agricultural products and global buyers seeking reliable sourcing partners.
Located in the heart of Khari Baoli, one of largest spice trading markets, H.A. IMPEX benefits from strong supplier networks, extensive market knowledge, and direct access to high-quality products. Our company works closely with trusted farmers, processors, and manufacturers to ensure that every product meets international quality and safety standards.


Our focus extends beyond simply supplying products. We strive to build long-term relationships based on trust, consistency, and customer satisfaction. Every order is handled with professionalism and attention to detail, ensuring timely delivery and complete peace of mind for our clients.
