Who We Are

Trusted Exporter of Premium Indian Spices

H.A. IMPEX is a leading Import-Export company based in Delhi, India, specializing in the global trade of premium-quality whole spices, crushed spices, and ground spices.

Established in 2026 and led by Mr. Hitesh Aggarwal Our company is committed to delivering authentic Indian flavors to international markets while maintaining the highest standards of quality, purity, and food safety.

Located in the famous spice trading hub of Khari Baoli, Delhi, we work closely with trusted growers and suppliers to source the finest spices. Through efficient logistics, competitive pricing, and transparent business practices, we proudly serve importers, wholesalers, distributors, and food manufacturers across global markets.
